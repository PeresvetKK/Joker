Put on Happy face

An alternative history of the formation of Batman's main enemy - the Joker. The life of Arthur (Joaquin Phoenix) is unremarkable. He lives with a sick mother, moonlights as a clown and suffers constant bullying from bullies. He was taught to smile from childhood, but his smile is a fit of uncontrollable laughter caused by brain injury. Mental problems, the cruelty of others and a terrible secret that has been hidden from Arthur all his life, turn him into the most desperate villain in Gotham City. But is he himself to blame...
